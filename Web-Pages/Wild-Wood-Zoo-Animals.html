Carnivores – Fierce Hunters of the Wild

Strong • Sharp • Fearless • Predators !



- African Lion-

Known as the "King of the Jungle," lions are powerful predators that live in prides. With their majestic manes and mighty roar, they symbolize strength and leadership.

-Bengal Tiger-

This striped big cat is native to the forests of India. Tigers are solitary and stealthy hunters, admired for their beauty, power, and grace.

-Gray Wolf (Canis lupus)-

The most widespread and well-known wolf species. Gray wolves are found in various habitats and are known for their pack behavior. Many subspecies fall under this category.



-Himalayan Black Bear-

With a thick black coat and a distinctive white chest mark, this bear is found in forested mountains. It’s an excellent climber and prefers fruits, honey, and roots.

-Leopard-

Leopards are sleek, spotted hunters known for their agility and strength. They can drag prey up trees and blend into their surroundings with incredible camouflage.

-Saltwater Crocodile (Crocodylus porosus)-

The largest living reptile in the world, found in Southeast Asia and Australia. Can live in both freshwater and saltwater. Often kept in specialized enclosures due to their size.







Herbivores – Gentle Plant Eaters

Peaceful • Leafy • Calm • Grazers !



-African Elephant-

The gentle giant of the zoo, elephants feed on leaves, fruits, and bark. They are known for their intelligence and strong family bonds.

- Masai Giraffe-

These towering animals use their long necks to munch on treetop leaves, especially acacia. Their calm nature makes them zoo favorites.

-Plains Zebra-

Zebras graze on open grasslands and use their stripes as camouflage. They live in groups and are always watchful.



-Mountain Goat-

Agile and playful, mountain goats love climbing and nibbling on shrubs and grass. They're common in petting zoos for kids.

-White-Tailed Deer -

Graceful and alert, deer feed on leaves, fruits, and soft plants. Their antlers and swift movements make them fascinating.

-American Bison-

Massive and strong, bison graze across open fields. Once endangered, they now thrive in protected zoo environments.







Aquatic Animals – Life Beneath Waves

Graceful • Cool • Deep • Swimmers !

-Bottlenose Dolphin-

Smart and playful, dolphins communicate through clicks and whistles. They are known for their high intelligence and friendly behavior with trainers and visitors.

- Green Sea Turtle-

These gentle swimmers graze on seagrass and algae. With their smooth shells and peaceful nature, they’re a calm presence in aquarium exhibits.

-Clownfish-

Bright orange with white stripes, clownfish live in sea anemones for protection. They're small, colorful, and loved by children and adults alike.



-Sand Tiger Shark-

A powerful predator with a fearsome look, yet calm in captivity. Found in large aquarium tanks, it plays an important role in marine education.

-Sea Otter -

Furry and active, sea otters float on their backs and crack open shells with rocks. They are a big attraction due to their playful habits.

-African Penguin-

These flightless birds swim gracefully underwater. Their tuxedo-like appearance and social waddling make them a crowd favorite in aquatic sections of zoos.
