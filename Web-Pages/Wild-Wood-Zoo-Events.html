World Wildlife Day

On World Wildlife Day, our zoo organizes interactive educational talks, wildlife conservation exhibits, and live animal shows to raise awareness about endangered species and the importance of protecting biodiversity. Visitors of all ages can learn how they can contribute to saving the wild.








Adopt an Animal Drive

This unique program allows visitors to symbolically adopt a zoo animal and support its care and enrichment. By participating, donors help cover food, medical care, and habitat improvement while receiving special certificates and updates on their adopted animal.








Zoo Clean-Up Campaign

Our Zoo Clean-Up Campaign invites volunteers and nature lovers to help us maintain clean and healthy enclosures for the animals. This hands-on initiative not only improves the environment but also fosters a sense of responsibility and connection to wildlife.








Animal Feeding Sessions

Feeding sessions offer a close-up experience where visitors can watch zookeepers feed animals while learning about their diets, behavior, and habitat needs. These sessions are both educational and exciting, especially for kids and families.
